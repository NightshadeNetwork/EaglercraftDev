What is Eaglercraft?

Eaglercraft is a fully-fledged version of Minecraft in your browser. It has all items in Minecraft versions Alpha 1.2-Release 1.8.8, and play community-made servers, too! You can play Eaglercraft on a phone as well, and our site has its own mobile UI. Eaglercraft supports all modern web browsers, so you can play Minecraft anytime, anywhere. Play Eaglercraft now on eaglercraft.dev by clicking the play button in your bottom right.

How to Get Started with Eaglercraft

  1. Open your web browser and visit Our Website. (Or download an offline Eaglercraft download)
  2. Navigate to the settings tab, and select your Eaglercraft options such as client, client theme, etc.
  3. Hit the play button in the bottom right to start playing Eaglercraft.
