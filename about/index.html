Eaglercraft.dev: About Us?

Welcome to Eaglercraft.dev. We're here because apparently, running a decent Minecraft community is rocket science for some people.

The Problem

The Eaglercraft community is a masterclass in how to run things into the ground. It's almost impressive how they've managed to take a game about building and turn it into a showcase of destruction. Bravo.

The Idiocy (In Detail!)

Arbitrary Banning

Imagine giving a toddler a loaded gun. Now imagine that gun bans people from their "Community Discord". That's the current state of moderation. It's like they're running a competition for "Fastest Way to Kill a Community" and going for gold, even though they rigged it already.

Suppression of New Ideas

You'd think new ideas were radioactive given how quickly they're shut down. God forbid someone suggests an improvement - that might require the admins to actually do something other than flex their ban muscles. Turns out it's just love handles, unless jitter-clicking Ctrl-V to paste ?ban therealserver. If they haven't, maybe this is the time to.

Hostility Towards Newcomers

Their welcome wagon is more like a welcome guillotine. Nothing says "thriving community" like treating newcomers as if they're carrying the plague. It's a bold strategy - let's see how driving away all new blood works out for them. Even if people are moronic, they're still people.

Lack of Transparency

Every "third-party launcher" or client all have their own private source code, which is just AI-Generated Grade-A (Tier S) Bullshit. Want a share of the pie? Ask GPT-4o to make you a Minecraft Launcher using HTML5, CSS3, and then watch as you magically see the same website you were staring at in disgust an hour ago.

Our "Revolutionary" Approach (Also Known as Basic Competence)

We've embarked on the apparently Herculean task of not being complete idiots. I know, contain your shock.

Here's our groundbreaking strategy:

  • Transparency: We will share our code if prompted! This is to help those who want to make their own client.
  • Support for Innovation: New ideas don't send us into a panic. We're brave like that.
  • Actual Help: We answer questions in English, not in riddles or grunts. The latter is common among those who partake in the other mainstream community.
  • Fair Moderation: We don't ban people for existing. The bar is low, but we're clearing it.

What We're Offering (Besides a Headache-Free Experience)

Brace yourselves for these mind-blowing offerings:

  • Client Hosting: We'll host your Eaglercraft client, granted that either we or the users trust it.
  • Server Integration: Get your server on our lists. We promise not to lose it in the couch cushions.
  • Development Support: Our source code isn't public, but we'll help you. Just give us a mention. We're not asking for your firstborn, just a little credit.
  • A Community That's Not a Dumpster Fire: We've set the bar so low it's a tripping hazard in Hell, yet here we are, limbo dancing with the devil.

The Long-Term Plan (Yes, We Actually Have One)

We're going to keep updating, improving, and generally not screwing things up. It's a novel approach in these parts, I know. We might even listen to feedback - don't faint from the shock.

What to Expect

With any luck, and a generous application of common sense, we might just create a community that doesn't make you want to gouge your eyes out. Expect fewer random bans, less idea suppression, and a marked decrease in the urge to scream into the void.

Fair warning: exposure to the general Eaglercraft community may still cause spontaneous facepalming and loss of faith in humanity. We're good, but we're not miracle workers.

Just want to have some fun? Come for the game, stay for the rare pleasure of not wanting to hurl your computer out the window.
