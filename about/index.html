About EaglercraftDev

Last Updated: July 16, 2024


Welcome to EaglercraftDev! Glad you dropped by. Our mission is to provide a stable, secure, and ad-free platform hosting both official and third-party Eaglercraft clients.

Our Vision

EaglercraftDev aims to be the go-to resource for access to Eaglercraft, as we host a wide variety of clients, with a client listing.

What We Offer

  • Ease of Access: Access to both official and third-party Eaglercraft clients in one place.
  • Stable Archiving: We maintain backups, offline downloads, and more to ensure that no client goes forgotten, etc, etc, etc. (Should I throw in a few more?)
  • Ad-Free Experience: Enjoy uninterrupted browsing and gameplay without any advertisements. This will not change, we have no plans to add ads as they can be malicious, etc.
  • Safety First: All clients are vetted for security to protect our users.

Why EaglercraftDev?

We here at EaglercraftDev pride ourselves on our features:

  • Free client hosting - We will host your client on request.
  • Long-term preservation of Eaglercraft versions (unless the creators of closed-source clients request that we remove them.)
  • User-friendly, ad-free interface
  • Rigorous safety checks on all hosted clients (Probably Unneeded, but I digress)

Our Process

We follow the following "rules" to ensure that our service remains online, safe, and fun for you.

  1. Collection: We gather both official and notable third-party Eaglercraft clients. Send us an email to request a client.
  2. Vetting: Our "team" thoroughly examines each client for potential security risks. We ensure that it doesn't log commands, typed messages, or even mine crypto in your browser.
  3. Archiving: This site is purely for archival purposes, approved clients are securely stored in our stable archive.
  4. Presentation: Clients are made available through our user-friendly interface.
  5. Maintenance: Regular updates and checks ensure the continued safety and functionality of all clients.

Safety Measures

Our Commitment to Your Security

While we strive to ensure the safety of all hosted clients, we recommend users to:

  • Always use the latest version of your preferred browser. (Which should be Firefox...)
  • Keep your operating system and antivirus software up to date. (Do I even need to add this? If you're EoL, get a patcher (if it's trusted) please.)
  • Be cautious when using third-party clients and report any suspicious behavior to maintainers on our Discord.
