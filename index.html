Eaglercraft Launcher

This website is in Development. We aren't currently finished, but we hope that our service will eventually become more usable and more informative.

We're not some skidded "launcher for Eaglercraft," instead this is a project built from the ground-up to provide a cleaner, safer experience when attempting to play/download Eaglercraft.

What is Eaglercraft?

Eaglercraft is a fully-fledged version of Minecraft in your browser. It has all items in Minecraft versions Alpha 1.2-Release 1.8.8, and plays community-made servers, too! You can play Eaglercraft on a phone as well, and our site has its own mobile UI. Eaglercraft supports all modern web browsers, so you can play Minecraft anytime, anywhere. Play Eaglercraft now on eaglercraft.dev by clicking the play button in your bottom right.

How to Get Started with Eaglercraft

  1. Open your web browser and visit Our Website. (Or download an offline Eaglercraft download)
  2. Navigate to the settings tab, and select your Eaglercraft options such as client, client theme, and so on.
  3. Hit the play button in the bottom right in order to start playing Eaglercraft.
